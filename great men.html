GREAT MEN FROM GOALPARA

Notable people from Goalpara include acclaimed actors Adil Hussain and Ashraful Haque, activist Birubala Rabha, spiritual leader Bhakti Ballabh Tirtha, and freedom fighter Leela Roy. Other notable figures are singer Abbas Uddin Ahmed, architect Jugal Kishore Choudhury, and social activist Kati Ram Rabha.

Adil Hussain: A renowned actor known for his roles in both Bollywood and international films, including Star Trek.

Ashraful Haque: A Bollywood actor who studied at the National School of Drama in Delhi.

Birubala Rabha: A social activist who campaigns against witchcraft and witch-hunting in India.

Bhakti Ballabh Tirtha: A spiritual leader and master.

Leela Roy: A prominent Indian independence activist and member of the Constituent Assembly.

Abbas Uddin Ahmed: A famous singer from the region.

Jugal Kishore Choudhury: A modernist architect.

Kati Ram Rabha: A social activist, freedom fighter, and pioneer of the Rabha society in South Kamrup.

Jatindra Narayan Das: A freedom fighter and the first MLA from the INC in the Dhubri district.

Baharul Islam: An actor and theatre personality.
